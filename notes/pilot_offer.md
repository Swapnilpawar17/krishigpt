# KrishiGPT – Pilot Offer (v1)

## Who is this for?

- 2–3 small/medium **agri retailers / FPOs / input companies**
- Focus: [YOUR REGION / STATE] (e.g. Western Maharashtra)

## What is included?

- **Duration:** 10–15 days (free pilot)
- **Channels:** 
  - Web app (KrishiGPT chat + dosage calculator)
  - (Optional) WhatsApp bot, if they want to test it
- **Crops covered (initial):**
  - [CROP 1] (e.g. Cotton)
  - [CROP 2] (e.g. Tomato)
  - [CROP 3] (optional, e.g. Onion)
- **Languages:** Hindi + [YOUR REGIONAL LANGUAGE] (e.g. Marathi)

## What KrishiGPT will do in the pilot?

- Answer farmer questions about:
  - Diseases & pests (IPM-first, then safe chemicals)
  - Fertilizers & dosage (with built-in dosage calculator)
  - Basic irrigation and government schemes (PM‑KISAN, PMFBY, KCC)
- Be available 24×7 for their staff (web) and optionally for selected farmers (WhatsApp)

## What we expect from the partner?

- 1–3 staff members use KrishiGPT during the pilot
- At least **20–30 real farmer questions** asked through the system
- 10–15 minutes feedback call at the end (what worked / what did not)

## Simple success criteria

We will consider the pilot successful if:

1. Their team says KrishiGPT:
   - Saves time on repeated questions, **or**
   - Helps avoid dosage mistakes

2. They are willing to:
   - Continue using it **or**
   - Try a bigger paid pilot in the next season
